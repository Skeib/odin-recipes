Lasagna Recipe

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients list

  • Besciamella
  • 1l Tomato Sauce
  • 500g Beef's Minced Meat
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 500g of fresh lasagna noodles
  • oregano
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • peperoncino
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Slice the onion and the garlic clove
  2. Add what sliced to a pan with 3 tablespoons of olive oil and let all fry till an almost brown color
  3. Add the minced meat and some water and cook for 1-2 minutes till the meat is not red anymore
  4. Add the Tomato Sauce
  5. Add some oregano, 1 tablespoon of salt, some pepper and peperoncino
  6. Let the ragù cook for about 1 hour at low to half flame, add water if needed
  7. After the ragù is ready, let it cool for 30 min
  8. Take a baking tray and cover the base with lasagna noodles
  9. Put the ragù over the lasagna noodles
  10. Put the Besciamella over the ragù
  11. Add 3 more layers by following the 3 previous steps
  12. Bake it in the oven at 180°C for 45 minutes
  13. Optional: bake it a second time for 20-30 minutes the day after if you wat it more crispy
